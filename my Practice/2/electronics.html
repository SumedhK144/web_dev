Electronics Engineering Department

Welcome to the Electronics Engineering department. We focus on electronic circuits, systems, and communication technologies.

Courses Offered:

  • Digital Electronics

    Study digital circuits, logic gates, and system design.

  • Analog Electronics

    Learn about analog circuit design and applications.

  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers

    Study architecture and programming of microprocessors.

  • Communication Systems

    Learn about signal processing and communication technologies.

  • VLSI Design

    Study design of integrated circuits and semiconductor devices.
