Chemical Engineering Department

Welcome to the Chemical Engineering department. We specialize in chemical processes and transformations for industrial applications.

Courses Offered:

  • Chemical Process Engineering

    Study design and optimization of chemical processes.

  • Reaction Engineering

    Learn about chemical reaction mechanisms and reactor design.

  • Separation Processes

    Study methods of separating chemical mixtures.

  • Polymer Engineering

    Learn about polymer synthesis and processing methods.

  • Biochemical Engineering

    Study applications of chemical engineering in biological systems.
