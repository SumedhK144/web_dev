Mechanical Engineering Department

Welcome to the Mechanical Engineering department. We specialize in the design and manufacturing of mechanical systems.

Courses Offered:

  • Thermodynamics

    Study energy conversion and heat transfer principles.

  • Machine Design

    Learn about designing mechanical components and systems.

  • Manufacturing Processes

    Study various methods of manufacturing mechanical components.

  • Fluid Mechanics

    Learn about behavior of fluids and their applications in engineering.

  • Robotics and Automation

    Study principles of robotics and automated manufacturing systems.
