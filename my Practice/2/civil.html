Civil Engineering Department

Welcome to the Civil Engineering department. We train future engineers in structural design and infrastructure development.

Courses Offered:

  • Structural Engineering

    Study the analysis and design of structures to withstand loads and forces.

  • Transportation Engineering

    Learn about design and management of transportation systems.

  • Geotechnical Engineering

    Study soil mechanics and foundation design principles.

  • Environmental Engineering

    Learn about water and waste management systems.

  • Construction Management

    Study principles of project management in construction.
